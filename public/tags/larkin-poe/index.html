The best albums of 2018

So the top 3 albums I heard in 2018 were - 3 Telltale Signs by Sobs - https://sobs.bandcamp.com/album/telltale-signs ‘Sobs delivers a set of songs that detail the frustrations and melancholy of romance, but instead of gloomy indie rock they wrap these tales up in infectious pop gems.’ 2 Dunes by Dunes (NZ) - https://www.facebook.com/dunesofficial ‘If a horse was a pop song this is what it would sound like.‘﻿ 1 Venom and Faith by Larkin Poe - http://www.larkinpoe.com/ ’ This isn’t your basic Stevie Ray Vaughan-styled blistering blues rock. It’s far more primal, malicious, and unsettling.’ (and Larkin Poe are gigging in Glasgow soon…) nice year 2018.
