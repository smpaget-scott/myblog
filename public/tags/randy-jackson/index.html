FAME(ous Jacksons)

I went with some of my family to see the musical FAME at the Kings theatre on Monday evening. it was okay, with several funny bits which made me laugh. anyway one of the charactors, Tyrone Jackson sings a song about how famous he is going to be and he starts to list off other famous Jacksons. . Randy Jackson Jesse Jackson Michael Jackson Mahalia Jackson got me thinking, surely their are more famous Jacksons that this list would suggest? Who is your favourite Jackson? any suggestions? Some starters for discussion Colin Jackson Miss Jackson (from the eponymous OutKast song) Samuel L Jackson Joe Jackson Leon Jackson or of course the famous (of infamous) Darren Jackson Tito Jackson (This was my favourite suggestion, for his work with both the Jackson5 and the wizard of Oz, not to mention his work with The Lone ranger)
