Oasis ♡ Cliff

Possibly my favourite story of the year so far. The NME is reporting that fans of Sir Cliff Richard are shocked/amazed at the brazen way in which Oasis have commandeered his 1976 hit “Devil Woman” to transform into a track called “The Turning” on the new Oasis album. Cliff Richard - Devil Woman Having listened to both I would say that there are remarkable similarities, which seem highly coincidental. Although i did hear radio 1 play a song from current hit parade which sounds very similar to an old hit. (Not including the Kid Rock Sweet Home Alabama nightmare).when I remember it i will pass it on. PS Loving the Pink song.
