rating the Album of the Year

various magazines have decided to anounce their top music albums of the year. I like to go through the list, count how many albums i have on each list to see how in tune i am with each magazine. Q magazine top 50 list Klaxons - Myths of the Near Future The National - Boxer The Decemberists - The Crane Wife Beirut - The Flying Club Cup Spoon - Ga Ga Ga Ga Ga Bright Eyes - Cassadaga Björk - Volta LCD Soundsystem - Sound of Silver Rilo Kiley - Under the Blacklight Modest Mouse - We Were Dead Before the Ship Even Sank Rufus Wainwright - Release the Stars Radiohead - In Rainbows The White Stripes - Icky Thump Arcade Fire - Neon Bible Paste magazine top 100 list (minus those above) Stars – In Our Bedroom After The War The Fiery Furnaces - Widow City Devendra Banhart - Smokey Rolls Down Thunder Canyon Do Make Say Think - You, You’re A History In Rust The New Pornographers - Challengers Okkervil River - The Stage Names St. Vincent - Marry Me Apples In Stereo - New Magnetic Wonder The Broken West - I Can’t Go On I’ll Go On Andrew Bird - Armchair Apocrypha Of Montreal - Hissing Fauna, Are You The Destroyer? Over The Rhine - The Trumpet Child Black Lips - Good Bad Not Evil Josh Ritter - The Historical Conquests of Josh Ritter Amy Winehouse - Back to Black Iron & Wine - The Shepherd’s Dog Band of Horses - Cease to Begin uncut magazine top 50 list (minus those above) Maps - We Can Create Steve Earle - Washington Square Serenade Feist - The Reminder Super Furry Animals - Hey Venus! Battles - Mirrored LCD Soundsystem - Sound of Silver having all this music does make me question if i have actually heard it all. I currently am most in tune with paste magazine. but they are the only ones so far to have Over the rhine in the top 100 so no doubt they would be my favourites. The other issue is how to list or rate all this music. do you go for a simple top 10 or top 50 I am toying with the idea of using the system used at the International Mathematical Olympiad, and awarding albums nothing, bronze, silver or Gold awards. but unsure about the critera for this type of marking system.
