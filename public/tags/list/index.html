Films for youth work

On the plane to Australia I had the chance to catch a few films, including the recent film “Sing street”. The film centres on a group of young people being young people in Ireland, during the nineteen eighties. As I watched this I thought this is a great film. Then I wondered if this would be a great film to use in a youth work setting. I think so, and at the end of the film I started a small list of films that say something about being young that I would show a group of 12-15 year olds. the list currently is. Gregory’s Girl 1981 E.T. 1982 The Breakfast club 1985 Clueless 1995 Romeo & Juliet 1996 10 things I hate about you 1999 Ping Pong 2002 Napoleon Dynamite 2004 Pitch Perfect 2012 Sing Street 2016 What should I add or take away?
