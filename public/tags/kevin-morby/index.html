"The Best 0f 2020" from Laconic Hegemony, side A (vol 17) & side B (vol 18), a #mixtape for you.

The new Laconic Hegemony mixtape is now out. Laconic Hegemony is a small mixtape I put out with my favourite songs of the past year annually. You can listen to all, some or none of it. It is your choice. All these songs have been songs which have accompanied me over 2020 and mean something to me in some way, (and I should point out, 2020 what a year it has been). I am aware they do not currently mean anything to you, hopefully that can change. Think of it as two sides of musical goodness to accompany your work, rest and play! If you are so inclined, let me know what you like from the mixtapes. https://www.mixcloud.com/sch3lp/vol20-the-best-of-2020-part-one/ https://www.mixcloud.com/sch3lp/vol21-the-best-of-2020-part-two/ Technically the Laconic Hegmony mixtapes are hosted by mixcloud.com who provide the free widget above, an app for streaming on your smart phone or even using a website, (A website, can you imagine!) The reason I use mixcloud.com is they are free for everyone, (which is important), and also they use their advertising income to pay some royalties to the featured artists. I have bought the music from the artists already, I like the idea that the artist will make something from this randomly being played on the internet. Streaming income is not a lot of money, so if you hear something you like. buy it directly from the artists if you can.
