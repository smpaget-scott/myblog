"Decade" from Laconic Hegemony, side A (vol 17) & side B (vol 18), a #mixtape for you.

December 21, 2019 · 4 min · admin

The new Laconic Hegemony mixtape “Decade” is now out. Laconic hegemony is a small mixtape I put out with my favourite songs of the past year annually however, this is a new mixtape of some of my favourite songs from the past decade. You can listen to all, some or none of it. It is your choice. All these songs have been songs which have accompanied me over the decade of the 2010’s and mean something to me in some way. I am aware they do not currently mean anything to you hopefully that can change. Think of it as two sides of 1hr30mins of goodness to accompany your work rest and play! If you are so inclined, let me know what you like from the mixtapes. https://www.mixcloud.com/sch3lp/a-decade-of-song-side-a-vol17/ https://www.mixcloud.com/sch3lp/a-decade-of-song-side-b-vol18/ Technically the Laconic Hegmony mixtapes are hosted by mixcloud.com who provide the free widget above, an app for streaming on your smart phone or even using a website, (A website, can you imagine!) The reason I use mixcloud.com is they are free for everyone, (which is important), and also they use their advertising income to pay some royalties to the featured artists. I guess it is not a lot of money but I like the idea that the artist will make something from this randomly being played on the internet.
