Album of the Year 2024 – The long list

December 15, 2024 · 4 min · admin

As you no doubt know, I select my album of the year every year. This is of no interest to most people but keeps me amused. To match $potify wrapped or whatever, I create a long list and attempt to whittle the list down to 3 top albums to be unveiled within the first week in January. This year is no different. I have enjoyed listening to lots of albums during 2024. In this list, you will also find something to enjoy if you venture that way.
