God's Eye View

I love this image, It is part of a collection called God’s eye view. an attempt to give a Google earth type view of four happenings in the bible. the four are the crucification as shown above, moses crossing the red sea, Noah and Adam and Eve. The images were created by an Australian collective called The Glue Society who look like they get to have some good fun during their work. the blog at creative review has the images and links to artists and the art exhibition it was commissioned for. nice so here’s another one. Creative Review, the world’s leading monthly magazine for visual communications technorati tags - The Glue Society, Creative Review & God’s Eye View

various magazines have decided to anounce their top music albums of the year. I like to go through the list, count how many albums i have on each list to see how in tune i am with each magazine.
