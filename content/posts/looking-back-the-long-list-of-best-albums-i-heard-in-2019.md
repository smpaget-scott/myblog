Here is the long list of the best music album of 2019. The rules are, it must be music I have heard in 2019, but the album does not need to have been released in 2019. No guarantee beyond this of anything but I that I liked the album.
[Blankenberge](https://blankenberge.bandcamp.com) \- More (russia)  
[Boygenius](https://xboygeniusx.bandcamp.com) \- boygenius (US)  
[Brighde Chaimbel](https://www.brichaimbeul.com)\- The Reeling (Scotland)  
[Buddy & Julie Miller](https://www.buddymiller.com) \- Breakdown on 20th Ave South (US)  
[Cate le Bon](http://www.catelebon.com) \- Reward (Wales)  
[Chain Wallet](https://chainwallet.bandcamp.com) \- No Ritual (US)  
[The Chemical Brothers](https://www.thechemicalbrothers.com) \- No Geography (England)  
[Courtney Marie Andrews](https://www.courtneymarieandrews.com) \- May Your Kindness Remain (US)  
[Filthy Friends](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Filthy_Friends) \- Invitation (US)  
[Flares on Film](https://flaresonfilm.bandcamp.com/releases) \- Naive Songs (Italy)  
[Funeral Advantage](http://www.funadv.org) \- Nectarine (US)  
[The Honey Dewdrops](https://www.thehoneydewdrops.com) \- Anyone Can See (US)  
[Jamila Woods](http://www.jamila-woods.com) \- Legacy! Legacy! (US)  
[Jennah Bell](http://www.jennahbell.com) \- Anchors and Elephants (US)  
[Joel Ansett](http://joelansett.com) \- A place I knew before (US)  
[Leah Nobel](https://www.leahnobel.com) \- Running in borrowed shoes (US)  
[Leslie Stevens](https://www.lesliestevensmusic.com) \- Sinner (US)  
[Long Beard](https://long-beard.bandcamp.com) \- Means to me (US)  
[Low Roar](http://www.lowroarmusic.com) \- ross. (Iceland)  
[Lucy Dacus](https://lucydacus.com) \- Historian (US)  
[Micheal Franti & Spearhead](https://michaelfranti.com)\- Stay Human vol. II (US)  
[Oso Oso](https://osoosoband.bandcamp.com) -Basking in the glow (US)  
[Over the Rhine](https://www.overtherhine.com) \- Love and Revelation (US)  
[Purple Pilgrims](http://www.purplepilgrims.net) \- Perfumed earth (NZ)  
[Rachel Sermanni](https://rachelsermanni.co.uk) \- So It Turns (Scotland)  
[Rainbow Girls](https://www.rainbowgirlsmusic.com)\- Give the People what they want (US)  
[Siamese Youth](https://newretrowave.bandcamp.com/album/electric-dreams) \- Electric Dreams (DE)  
[Tallies](https://tallies.bandcamp.com/album/tallies) \- Tallies (Canada)  
[Team Body](https://terrorbird.com/albums/dreamo) \- Dreamo (US)
I have managed to put all the albums onto a Spotify playlist for you all, should you want.
