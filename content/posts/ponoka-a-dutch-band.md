After my love affair with [At The Close Of Every Day,](http://www.atthecloseofeveryday.com/) whose excellent album [The Silja Symphony](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Silja-Symphony-Close-Every-Day/dp/B0002NCKEY/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=music&qid=1208976633&sr=8-1) is still one of my top 10 listens, ever. And obviously my pleasurable addiction to [I'm From Barcelona](http://www.imfrombarcelona.com/), and various swedish detours including [Hello Saferide](http://www.hellosaferide.com/) I have recently found a new dutch band to become excited about
[Ponoka](http://www.ponokamusic.com/news.php)  
[![](http://bp2.blogger.com/_AQL5BvjF3p8/SA990OyxF_I/AAAAAAAAARw/wZXDauriDd4/s400/ponoka-interweb.jpg)](http://bp2.blogger.com/_AQL5BvjF3p8/SA990OyxF_I/AAAAAAAAARw/wZXDauriDd4/s1600-h/ponoka-interweb.jpg)
Okay I haven't heard much of their music. but what i have heard i like quite alot. The band are a indie rock band from, Utrecht, with a male/female singing group and a number of good songs. They claim Lemmonheads, Sparklehorse, Eels and Pavement as influences. (I must get that eels best of album.) They are signed to [Volkoren](http://www.volkoren.com) the dutch independant label.
Their latest album is called Hindsight and in english. [The whole thing is being streamed live on Last.fm](http://www.last.fm/music/Ponoka/Hindsight) it sounds quite pleasent.
but the joy is perhaps more [lavish on their Youtube channel](http://www.youtube.com/user/ponokamusic). where they have some songs from the album and a new one, the exquisite Disconnected.
The band just strike me as a really cool band who have a good sound that could one day conquer the world!
anyway check them out
