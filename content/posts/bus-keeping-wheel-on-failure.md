On succhiehall street just at the turning next to the animal shop, this bus broke down. By Broke I mean the back wheel came off the bus, the driver here was just about to take this wheel and put it in the bus while he waited to be rescued. I felt sorry for him. He probably isn't the mechanic. It was also driving heavy rain as this happened. I felt sorry of the passsengers who had to wait in the rain for another bus. I felt sorry for the drivers who were stuck in traffic unexpectantly. I suppose I just felt sorry about the situation. I was walking, I got wet. I was sorry about that. My right contact lense moved off my eye, I was kinda blind. I was sorry about that. I was off work early and had got needed new clothes. I was glad about that.
