Recently our washing machine quit working.  
no really this was quite a big thing. Jacob demands a large wash load and our drying space in limited. also within the last 6 months the laundrette down the road has shut. bummer.  
[![](http://photos1.blogger.com/blogger/3339/480/320/100_0398.jpg)](http://photos1.blogger.com/blogger/3339/480/1600/100_0398.jpg)  
our old machine was a servis washer, the machine was reasonably cheap and worked well for the 3 and a half years we owned it. but recently it had become louder and more well ragged. our washer was dying to the point where it stopped working. We wnet that day to a large electical stockest and picked a new machine. as pictured below.
It's nice.  
it has a digital read out and tells how how long the wash has left. It even turns the kitchen blue if you turn the lights out!  
[![](http://photos1.blogger.com/blogger/3339/480/320/100_0402.jpg)](http://photos1.blogger.com/blogger/3339/480/1600/100_0402.jpg)  
we were told the machine would be in stock on saturday. (this was firday with a delivery date sometime around the wednesday.)  
we phoned on the saturday. the machine wasn't in stock yet but would be on sunday.
On the sunday we went paid for it. We were told the computer wasn't updated but the machine would be in stock on the monday.
we phoned on monday. not in stock today would be tomorrow call then  
we phoned on tuesday. not in stock today would be tomorrow call then  
we called on wednesday. not in stock today would be tomorrow call then  
we called on thursday. not in stock today would be tomorrow call then
My wife a bit annoyed called the shop we bought it from spoke to a girl who knew nothing. she asked to speak to the manager.suddenly the girl could call her back with a definate date when the machine would arrive.
she did and the machine arrived two weeks ago on Tuesday.
Is it good.
it is
