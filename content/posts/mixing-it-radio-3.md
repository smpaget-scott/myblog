I never really had thought of radio 3 as the place for New Music. Perhpas The big Billboards that carried the legen "IN NEW MUSIC WE TRUST" had convinced me that the BBC thought that Zane Lowe's Radio 1 show was the home of new music.
Imagine my surprise when I was surfing the web, (How 90's am I!), I found the detaisl of this wee radio show.
Mixing it goes out on a Friday evenings from 22.15hours on Radio 3 from the BBC. It has a good mix of lofi and cool artists with the avantgarde. Frankly at times it is so avantgarde thats it is painful. Reminds me for the episode of Spaced, where Brian meets an artist from his past - Vulva. Painful.
Anyway well worth a flick as there is really nothing else on the radio at that time of night for the drive home on fridays
