I guess I am just bummed about not going this year.   
But two young people I work with at a residential are going with their family.   
I hope they enjoy it  
I hope the love it  
I hope the festival is good to them.
I know I will miss it  
I love the going and the being a part of everything, and trying to do to much and around sunday afternoon just giving up and discovering things I would normally neverhave went near with a barge pole.
I like greenbelt.  
I like the creative ideas I get.
I like the rest and the quiet and lack of pressure to fufil the roles i normally have. I can be me.
My friends will love it.  
greenbelt does that to you
