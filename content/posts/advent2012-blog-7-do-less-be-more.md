Today was great day, as a family
we swam, we eat, we shopped, we went to a puppet show.
We forgot our jackets, we remembered how to get on. We annoyed each other. We were considerate of each other.
We were family. That was good.
This week has been a good week,
I have planned with co-conspiritors. A meeting where I was affirmed and encouraged despite the amount of work still to be done.
I have met old friends, talking chatting.
I have been out with my resposible adults. eating, drinking, talking, laughing and hearing things
I have done less and been more. This has been good.
