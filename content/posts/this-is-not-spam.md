recently the spam filter has been picking up some very complimentary spam in the old spam filter. this includes;-
"Viagra, this is the coolest site"
"Viagra, incredible site"
and the classic
"Yooo..
I kno it has nothing to do with what you wrote, but have you ever heard of \[some website address\] . They seems to promise free ringtones
PS. Dont be an ass, this is NOT spam ;) "
sorry it is the PS. Dont be an ass, this is not spam \[smileyface winking\] that really makes me laugh.
On a slightly more concerning issue it does appear that my website has an invigorating effect on the first two spammers of is Viagra now a new term for amazement. My brother did at once float the idea of turning "Homebase" into the verb for doing something not very well, kinda shoddily,
"oh look at that bookcase, its been homebased"
"you don't want to do an homebase"
"you did a homebase on that bookcase"
I think it could catch on you know!
