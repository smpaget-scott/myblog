I came home on Saturday to find that there was a CD I had ordered delivered to me. The CD is wonderful. I had downloaded the MP3 of The Innocence missions version of "Moon River" somewhere years ago or something.
The CD is wonderful. Karen Peris's voice is wonderfull. I just really like the idea that my child's first CD will be by [The Innocence Mission](http://www.theinnocencemission.com). Out of Interest the second CD will be one by [They Might Be Giants](http://www.giantkid.net/). Anyway. The thing I really dig about the CD is just the space which the voice, guitars, bass and other instruments create for each other. I think I may get into this dreamy music some more.
