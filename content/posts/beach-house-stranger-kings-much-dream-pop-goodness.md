I am a sucker for some [dream pop](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dream_pop), and two new music releases of a dream pop type persuasion are soon to be in my possession, (well one on order and one being shipped from the USA). The first is the new album by [Beach House](http://www.beachhousebaltimore.com) called "Depression Cherry". I have this ordered without hearing anything from it yet, so it better be good... I am confident it will be, I loved the album "[Bloom](http://www.metacritic.com/music/bloom)" from 2012. It is a remarkably good album, so good it is one of the 38 albums I choose to carry with me on an MP3 device at all times. {{< youtube id="PBjxOeVLY3g" >}} The question is whether to buy tickets for Beach House's October 26th Glasgow concert or not. I suspect I will, but I am not sure how rock and roll it will be. (Below is a film Beach House made with performances of 4 songs from Bloom.){{< youtube id="lgD8vWIB8hs" >}} The second album I have bought is by a Californian dream pop practitioners called [Stranger Kings](https://www.facebook.com/strangerkings?fref=ts). You can listen to the album on band camp below.  They have gorgeous 80's peter hook influenced base work, dreamy guitars, and a beautiful voice on top, whats not to like right? {{< youtube id="_mCT68qeQJY" >}} mucho musical goodness.
