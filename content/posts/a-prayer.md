Iwrote a prayer for church yesterday. It went like this...
Praise God in the gym, the cold icy tundra and the car.  
Praise him for his perfectness, love, mercy, faithfulness and fairness,  
Praise him with overheard music from cars and headphones,  
Praise him with things of worth,  
Praise him with a street procession and a festival,  
Praise him with you. who you are and can be.  
Praise The Lord. Everything which can praise him.
Amen.
