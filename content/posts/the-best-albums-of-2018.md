So the top 3 albums I heard in 2018 were -
3
[![](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/a2831715091_10.jpg)](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/a2831715091_10.jpg)
Telltale Signs by Sobs - [https://sobs.bandcamp.com/album/telltale-signs](https://sobs.bandcamp.com/album/telltale-signs)
'[**Sobs** delivers a set of songs that detail the frustrations and melancholy of romance, but instead of gloomy indie rock they wrap these tales up in infectious pop gems.](http://fadeawayradiate.com/2018/08/sobs-telltale-signs/)'
2
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/27545313%5F894664284025135%5F2693528962537361247%5Fn.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
Dunes by Dunes (NZ) - [https://www.facebook.com/dunesofficial](https://www.facebook.com/dunesofficial)
['If a horse was a pop song this is what it would sound like.'﻿](https://noisey.vice.com/en_au/article/vb4nb8/nz-sister-duo-dunes-create-pop-music-inspired-by-horses)  
1   
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/venomandfaith.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
Venom and Faith by Larkin Poe - [http://www.larkinpoe.com/](http://www.larkinpoe.com/)
' [This isn’t your basic Stevie Ray Vaughan-styled blistering blues rock. It’s far more primal, malicious, and unsettling.](https://americansongwriter.com/2018/11/larkin-poe-venom-faith/)'
(and Larkin Poe are [gigging in Glasgow soon...](https://www.songkick.com/concerts/36098564-larkin-poe-at-oran-mor))
_nice year 2018._
