I Have been thinking about death recently. [Lamb](http://lambstar.net/home.html) the band have split up. (I think it is final.)I was listening to the Best Kept secrets album when the the CD player shifted from track 3 to track 4.
Gorecki.
The beginning of the song caught my attention. It is used to great effect in the film Moulain Rouge. Nicole Kidman, no less, sings it. The lyric caught me straight away. The mix of love and death got me thinking in a big way. In 2 weeks at Lochend school's SU I am doing Death. I volunteered for it. So my Death antennae are set to full.
