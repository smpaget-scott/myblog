Youth work has caused me to think.
why  
\- would you not want to engage with young people?  
\- would you not change?  
\- not be more passionate?  
\- would you not take care of your staff?  
\- would you not take care of the young people you work with?  
\- would you be unsafe when you can avoid it?  
\- would you show a lack of commitment?
questions i ask myself in the stillness.  
answers on a postcard.
I wanna do better,  
I wanna try harder,  
I want to believe down to the letter.
