As you no doubt know, I select my album of the year every year. This is of no interest to most people but keeps me amused.
To match $potify wrapped or whatever, I create a long list and attempt to whittle the list down to 3 top albums to be unveiled within the first week in January. This year is no different. I have enjoyed listening to lots of albums during 2024. In this list, you will also find something to enjoy if you venture that way. You will have heard of some people on this list, and perhaps others you will never have heard of.
