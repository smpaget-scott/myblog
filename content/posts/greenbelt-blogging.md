[Greenbelt](http://www.greenbelt.org.uk/), in association with [CMS](http://www.cms-uk.org/), is running a competition for bloggers. The prize is a trip to africa next summer. The competition is to write some posts, on the [greenbelt blog](http://www.greenbelt.org.uk/gbblog/), about greenbelts main aims of Faith, Justice and Arts. 3 blog posts relating to these subjects using a maximum of 200 words.
I have entered (you need to be within 18-30 to be eligable) but think I will get absolutely beaten out of sight. STill I am the only one to have posted anything so far so I guess I am in the lead currently.
