Christmas soon and yes a new selection has appeared.  
all wrapped nicely and with ribbon on and stuff.
in particular it sees the return of REM with Green, a great album, Steve Taylor with his 1992 album Squint which is just wonderful! even now.  
(I so know it all word perfect.) But balanced with new music by Kanye West and Clap Your Hands Say Yeah.
The weirdest album is the WAIWAN release Distraction form about 1992 as well.   
Waiwan is a DJ who DJ's in england. His album I would term ambient and has some fantastic songs on it. His record company website terms him as a Nu-Jazz artist.  
I am bummed if I know What that is!
