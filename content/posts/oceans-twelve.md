went to see ocean's twelve at "the odeanat the quay" on saturday, i kinda liked it but it felt a bit flat.
I mean it was funny in bits and i did care thatwhat happened ahd happened. But a bit flat compared with the first one.
Prehaps it was a feeling of failure that the film inspires. I didn't really see how the twist had happened but i was pretty sure there was going to be one
Brad pitt was good,
George Clooney was good,
Julia Roberts was funny,
Matt Damon definatly gives the best performance.
The Bruce Willis cameo was also pretty unexpected.
Not bad but most of the funny lines were already within the trailer which has been kicking about for a couple of months now.
overall 6.5 out of ten
