Mark Kermode, genius film reviewer, reviewing Disney's film "Barnyard".
> Ladies and Gentlemen,
> quite apart from the fact it is a rubbish movie, doesn't make any sense, has no story.
> I am proud to tell you that it is, as far as i can tell, the first
> transexual, lesbian, anthropomorphic, digital, animal animation for kids.
>
> That is pretty much the only thing it has going for it.
Mark Kermode is currently the provider of the best 40 minutes of radio available in the UK every week. he is the regular reviewer on the film review section, friday afternoons on BBC FiveLive, during the Simon Mayo show.
