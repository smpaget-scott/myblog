so the call back session is over and the class is no more.
[Strathclyde University's](http://www.strath.ac.uk/) [Post Graduate Certificate in Community Education](http://www.strath.ac.uk/Departments/CommunEdu/) course has been fun (or stressful,annoying, excellent, lousy and weird at some points). I mean I got exactly what I expected from it. being challenged, getting time to think, reflect, learn, and challenging.
the last night ended up in partick with in depth conversations of the state of socialism, feminism, post modernism, religion, argument about starting a real wage campaign in Glasgow, why Christianity may or may not be true. How I am in the top three Christians one of my course mates knows. and finally which celebrity you would like to eat you or would you like to be eaten by. kinda bizarre end to the evening but at the end there were only a few who had had a good time.
It was a good time and more thought on this to follow.
