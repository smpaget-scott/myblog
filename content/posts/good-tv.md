This week sees the return of two of my favourite TV shows.
[![](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/bigbang_banner-300x113.jpg)](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/bigbang_banner.jpg)[The Big Bang Theory on E4](http://www.channel4.com/entertainment/tv/microsites/B/bigbangtheory/) \- 9pm tonight and Wednesdays following
then on friday  [channel 4](http://www.channel4.com/) sees [the return of the mighty The IT Crowd](http://www.channel4.com/entertainment/tv/microsites/I/itcrowd/) [![](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/the-it-crowd-300x234.jpg)](/blog/wp-content/uploads/2008/11/the-it-crowd.jpg)
Intresting both seem to have have a geeky element to them. both seem to be both embracing geekdom while still using the fact people are into stuff as a major comedy plot point.
I Guess this balances out my love of the lowest common denominator [Hole in the Wall](http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b00dr78v) on [BBc](http://www.bbc.co.uk/) and of course the near perfection of [Harry Hill's TV Burp](http://www.itv.com/Entertainment/comedy/HarryHillsTVBurp/default.html) on [ITV](http://www.itv.com/).
