Saw King Arthur on DVD tonight. It was for work as one of the young people I work with has this as his favourite film ever. (Until I was 18 mine was Top Gun or Waynes World!) The film was over 2 hours long but didn't seem to long at that length. The story was easy to follow and fight scenes were very gory. (Apparently we have the extended directors cut with 2 minutes more gore.) I can hardly sit through casualty so this was work. But I found it pleasant enough way to pass an evening. Now I just have to remember a bit about it so when I chat to my young friend on Friday I know what I am talking about. Nice bit about faith in it as well.
