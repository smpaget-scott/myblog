I went to see Somersault on Sunday. GOOD MOVIE, Somersault is a film that stays with you. It has a pace and feel that is similar to reality TV only it doesn't allow you to notice the concious editing as it goes along.
The film is the story of one girls adventure. Heidi seems about 17, she leaves home after her mum finds Heidi snogging her mothers boyfriend. She goes to a holiday town in austrailia where she has some coming of age type stuff. mostly it is about the difference between Love and Sex.
I liked this film immensely. It was funny, cool, sad and just connecting on a level which i found has stayed with me. Well worth the admission fee to see.
