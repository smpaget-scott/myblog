My wife went to hospital today for an overnight before they induce her tomorrow.
She arrived at 3pm I was able to stay in a very warm room until 6pm. I left and came back at visitiing time 7pm-8pm.
they needed to do some tests before tomorrow. Basically the baby is 10 days late tomorrow. So they have decided to induce. You kinda hope things will work out on their own but tomorrow they will kinda give the body and jumpstart to encourage labour to happen.
I am at home alone supposed to relax!  
I don't think i will be doing much relaxing tonight!
I suppose I am   
Nervous  
Scared  
Excited  
Fearful  
Anything except calm.
By tomorrow evening we should have a child. For the rest of my life I will have an influence on this person, survey indicate that in teenage years the biggest influence on a young persons life is their parents. Talk about responsability.
My wife and I laughed and Joked our way through visiting this evening.
The couple in the bed next to my wife looked about 16, the mother was being visited by her parents and their didn't seem to be a lot of laughing and joking going on. Infact the atmosphere was very tense. All the girl needed was support and the knowledge that forgiveness may not be immediate but that love was unconditional. Yet her parents were quiet. When the Boys arents arrived, things brigened up. The parent's didn't seem to know each other that well. But the conversation started between them. Even so the father of the girl joked about him giving her tough love with that kind of half humor half seriousness that the scots as a race do so well.
We don't know whats ahead of us.  
We know that respect it and laugh about it.
