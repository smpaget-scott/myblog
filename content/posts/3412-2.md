I forgot to put up my albums of the year
An [aggregate review site](https://www.metacritic.com/browse/albums/score/metascore/year?sort=desc&year_selected=2023) tells me that the top three albums of the year are
[Fountain Baby by Amarae](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/amaarae-fountain-baby/)
[Fly or Die Fly or Die Fly or Die (world war) by Jaimie Branch](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/jaimie-branch-fly-or-die-fly-or-die-fly-or-die-world-war/)
[Desire, I Want To Turn into You by Catherin Polachek.](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/caroline-polachek-desire-i-want-to-turn-into-you/)
While congratulations are due to all of these artists on their critical reception, my top 3 are based on my ears and my thoughts.

1- [Out and About by Lewsberg](https://lewsberg.bandcamp.com/album/out-and-about)
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Javelin%5FCover%5F%5F%5F1500%5Fpx%5F91c5bddd%5Fthumbnail%5F1024.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
2 - [Javelin by Sufjan Stevens](https://www.roughtrade.com/en-gb/product/sufjan-stevens/javelin)
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/Slowdive%5Feverythingisalive%5FCoverArt-1692991347-scaled-1.jpeg" alt="" caption="" >}}
3 - [Everything Is Alive by Slowdive](https://www.roughtrade.com/en-gb/product/slowdive/everything-is-alive-2)
Every year, I add one album to a list which echoes the Cannes Film Awards catergory of **_Prix un certain regard._** Those albums I like but regard uncertainly. I choose one a year of the albums, which means something to me, but perhaps I do not have the magic for someone else. This years addition is
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/a1475724699%5F16.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
[Romantic Piano by Gia Margaret.](https://giamargaret.bandcamp.com/album/romantic-piano)
Thanks, that is all.
