quality control as per my daughter
1\. Find item on floor
2\. Lift item off floor
3\. Shake item
4\. Bite item
5\. Hit item off floor see if it brakes
6\. Bite item again
7\. Shake Item
8\. Lose interest and discard item
...repeat until bedtime.
so far our toys seem to have passed the test!
