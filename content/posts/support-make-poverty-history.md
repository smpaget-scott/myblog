Hi I have been asked how to get one of the bands to support make poverty histroy for your website/ blog. This links you to [makepovertyhistory.org](http://www.makepovertyhistory.org), the UK part of the campaign. With the G8 meeting in Gleneagles in June this needs to be as wedly spread as possible. so here for everyone is the link
[http://www.makepovertyhistory.org/getinvolved/banners.shtml](http://www.makepovertyhistory.org/getinvolved/banners.shtml)
follow the link and highlight the java script you want, (right or left essentially)  
highlight, right click and copy.  
log into your blogger account.  
select the blog you want if you have more than one!  
click on template  
click on edit current  
scroll down until you find the instruction < body >  
scroll down until you see the instruction < div id="content" >  
click in the line below.  
right click and paste the script.  
click preview to see the results  
click save template changes to make the changes permenant.
there you are.  
well done
