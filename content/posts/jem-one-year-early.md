Last January I bought two albums on the internet. One was by Nellie McKay and is good. the other was By a Girl Called [Jem](http://www.jem-music.net/). All I knew was she had a great song called "They" on it. The album "Finally Woken arrived in the psot and I immediatley liked it. Jems voice is cool, the album sounded V.good.
anyway fast forward to last week. Ken Bruce on BBC radio Two has "They" by Jem as his single of the week. BBC Radio one has playlisted a certain song called "They" by Jem, A new version of the album is on sale at HMV and Virgin.
Hmmm  
I knew I was before my time but this proof is just ridiculous
