I have recently found four things which are making life more advance technology wise.
> Bloglines
Bloglines is a simple system. you sign up. then once in you surf as normal all the blogs you normally read.when you sign up you get a link for submitting webpages. you submit your favourite blogs to blog lines. you then delete the link from your favourites.   
Every submitted weblog is then in a blog aggrigator which gathers updates since you were last on line. so every morning when you check your must see websites! you click bloglines and it tells you which blogs have been updated and by how many posts. It makes better use of your time on the net. quite handy indeed.
bloglines website [::\[\[click here\]\]::](http://www.bloglines.com)  
> PBwiki
PBwiki is easy.  
Wiki is a piece of server software that allows users to freely create and edit Web page content using any Web browser. Wiki supports hyperlinks and has a simple text syntax for creating new pages and crosslinks between internal pages on the fly  
This site provides a small website for colaberation on one piece of work.  
I am playing with it with my own youthwork planning but intend to use it for the larger team youthwork planning.  
It doesn't need any fancy programming. just log in and write stuff.  
very simple.  
very effective.
pbwiki website [::\[\[click here\]\]::](http://pbwiki.com/)
