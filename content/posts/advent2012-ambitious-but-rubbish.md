Every year I valiantly attempt to mark to period of advent by blogging every day for a 40 day period. (Yes I take Sundays off). I like that I attempt this.
I like that I fail very often in this attempt.
I like that in the words of top gear I am, ambitious but rubbish.
I like that I can try and play, and enjoy panicking when I feel I have nothing left to say.
Also at the foot of each day will be the link to a video of lovely Christmas music for you to enjoy.
So here we go 40 days of blogging.
