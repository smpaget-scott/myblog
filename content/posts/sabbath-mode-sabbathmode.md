The new oven is shiny and beautiful. It even plays a tune when you turn it on and off. My interest was piqued when I saw in the user manual something called "Sabbath mode".
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG%5F0822.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
I have to admire their understanding of the sabbath as very strict, and also their attempt to relate that understanding to the outworking of a particular mode of an oven. As practical religious understanding goes, this oven is certainly interfaith.
