The new [Howies Catalogue](http://www.howies.co.uk) for summer hit our doorstep yesterday.  
it is full of lovely things which i could buy and seem to be calling me to buy. but perhaps when i get a pay rise it will be my playground
In the mean time two things to draw your attention to.
1 - [the joy image](http://www.howies.co.uk/images/cms/wallpaper/1600x1200/ws_85.jpg)  
as displayed above and downloadable as a desktop image.
2 - [the DO lectures](http://www.howies.co.uk/content.php?xSecId=92)  
these are basically a small gathering of around 75 invited guests to get together with the guys from Howies. During it some people will lecture, and ways to change will be planned. Plus there is a forest to explore.
The lectures are free but to be selected you need to apply in handwriting. Explaining what it is you plan to do. I have an idea what my application will include so we will see what happens.
