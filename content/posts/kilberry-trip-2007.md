I have started a [Bebo](http://www.bebo.com/Default.jsp) site for BCM, [Port Ban, Kilberry](http://www.portban.com/index.php?option=com_wrapper&Itemid=31) Camp. which I led this weekend. It was fun despite the gale force winds and driving rain.
It was good to be at the beach, to sit round fires, and get to spend time with new and familiar people. it grows the soul. grows life. allows some fragment of heaven to break through the grime and slime and stuff we put in the way of it.
The details of what we did and supporting websites and thoughts will be put up there in the blog for all the young people and leaders who were at the weekend. thanks to all who were there and helped at the weekend.
