Possibly my favourite story of the year so far.
[The NME](http://www.nme.com/news/cliff-richard/40247) is reporting that fans of [Sir Cliff Richard](http://www.cliffrichard.org/) are shocked/amazed at the brazen way in which [Oasis](http://www.oasisinet.com/) have commandeered his 1976 hit "Devil Woman" to transform into a track called "The Turning" on the new [Oasis](http://www.oasisinet.com/) album.
Cliff Richard - Devil Woman
Having listened to both I would say that there are remarkable similarities, which seem highly coincidental. Although i did hear radio 1 play a song from current hit parade which sounds very similar to an old hit.
