I saw Stuart a minister in glasgow today.
We were both out with family at Cardwell Bay garden centre near gourock on the west coast of scotland.
I didn't recognise him at first and I don't think he recognised me.
I did go up and say hello and had a chat with him while we were in the centre but it was weird how i wasn't sure it was him just due to the situation we were in.
sorry not weird just strange. It shows just how important context is within a relationship between people. work colleagues arn't meant to have their social life overlap yours. Then again Scotland Is Such a small plce i shouldn't really be surprised.
