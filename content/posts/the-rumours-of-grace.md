I am great full to the people at the “Christian history Almanac” podcast for letting me know that I share a bithday with Pulcheria, born in 399. I like this podcast and think you should try it.
I am very curious about their sign off each day. They use the same phase to sign off the podcast -
**... and remember that the rumors of grace, forgiveness, and the redemption of all things are true…. Everything is going to be ok.**
For who? When? What does this mean for everyone? I don’t think everything is going to be ok all the time, but perhaps the good news of Jesus is within an eschatological timescale (now but not yet) grace, forgiveness, and the redemption of all things are true...
