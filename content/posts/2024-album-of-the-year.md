An [aggregate review site](https://www.metacritic.com/browse/albums/score/metascore/year/filtered?year_selected=2024&distribution=&sort=desc&view=detailed) tells me that the top three albums of the year are
[Brat by Charli XCX](https://www.theguardian.com/music/article/2024/jun/06/charli-xcx-brat-review-insecurity-obliterating-anthems-by-pops-most-human-superstar)
[Songs for a Lost World by The Cure](https://thequietus.com/quietus-reviews/album-of-the-week/songs-of-a-lost-world-the-cure-review/)
[Absolute Elsewhere by Blood Incantation](https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/blood-incantation-absolute-elsewhere/)
While congratulations are due to all of these artists on their critical reception, my top 3 are based on my ears and my thoughts.  
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/a2042898822%5F10-1024x1024.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
1- [SHINBANGUMI by Ginger Root](https://www.gingerroot.com)
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/a1261557895%5F10.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
2 - [Seed of by Seed by Hayley Hendrickx](https://www.haley-heynderickx.com)
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/a2398709772%5F10-1-1024x1024.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
3 - [Below a Massive Dark Land by Naima Bock](https://www.naimabock.com/)
Every year, I add one album to a list which echoes the Cannes Film Awards catergory of **_Prix un certain regard._** Those albums I like but regard uncertainly. I choose one a year of the albums, which means something to me, but perhaps I do not have the magic for someone else. This years addition is
{{< figure src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/a1214950132%5F10.jpg" alt="" caption="" >}}
[Midwinter Swimmers by The Innocence Mission.](https://theinnocencemission.bandcamp.com/album/midwinter-swimmershttps://giamargaret.bandcamp.com/album/romantic-piano)
Thanks, that is all.
