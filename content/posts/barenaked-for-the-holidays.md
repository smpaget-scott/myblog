[The BareNaked Ladies](http://www.bnlmusic.com/) gig last night was awesome. It was very good indeed. I think it was really good and started the christmas season off well. I think it was possibly the most exciting gig I have been at. We were down the front on the right hand side. (Ed's side) I really think that the audience makes a real difference to the gig. Last night there was a real mix of people and a real mix of excitment levels. Barenaked Ladies fans have always been fanatical. (the gig last night sold out and the gig in glasgow on the "[everything to everyone](http://www.bnlmusic.com/music/default.php)" tour was the only British gig to completely sell out.
I think a band who can have fun, communicate with the crowd and provide plentyful opportunities to sing along will always be successful. Unfortunate about the Carling Academy being such a lousy venue. No spirt about the place, seats in the venue, air conditioning. I am sure that there are much better venues this band could play.
Great night out though.
thanks Barenaked Ladies you crazy canadians.
