Yesterday i learned a new word.  
It was used on an e-mail forum i am a memeber of, by the editor of [Third Way Magazine.](http://www.thirdway.org.uk) The word is...   
> "Dilettante"
so what was a Dilettante?
I actually went out and looked up the [dictionary.com website](http://dictionary.reference.com/wordoftheday/archive/2003/10/18.html) to find out.
And I am one.
Today though, I heard the word used on the radio. since then, I have had the smug self satisfaction of knowing a new word, and when hearing it in conversation I knew what they were on about.
Life long learning, it's great!
